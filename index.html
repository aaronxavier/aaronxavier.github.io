Aaron Xavier

Research Associate — Robotics & Autonomous Systems

Chair of Material Handling and Warehousing (FLW), TU Dortmund

I design and build autonomous multi-robot systems for intralogistics. At TU Dortmund I am developing a drone swarm platform on Crazyswarm2 and a series of multi-robot demonstrators that combine UAVs and ground robots with live warehouse management systems — work that sits at the boundary of robotics research and industrial deployment.

Before that I was at Robert Bosch GmbH, where I designed a behavior-tree-based deliberative architecture for mobile robot navigation and built LiDAR-camera sensor fusion pipelines for automotive perception. My background spans embedded systems, ROS2, computer vision, and full-stack robot software from microcontrollers to cloud messaging.
