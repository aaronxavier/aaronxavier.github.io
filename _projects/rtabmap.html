Spatial Mapping

An Evaluative Study of Spatial Map Building Algorithms

Studies the possibility of using RTAB-Map for a 3D mapping and reconstruction pipeline which can further be integrated into robotic systems for various applications. Simulated the system on ROS and Gazebo and implemented it on an NVIDIA Jetson TX2 and ZED2 on the laboratory UAV.
